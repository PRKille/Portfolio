About Me

Education

After graduating high school in 2005 I attended the University of Kansas to pursue a degree in Electrical Engineering. After my third year attending I realized that was not the path for me and decided to leave academia in an attempt to find my calling.

Work Experience

Not long after leaving school I began working in kitchens. It did not take more than a few weeks for me to realize this work was what I should be doing.

Some highlights of my career:

  • Slow Ride Roadhouse where I was often part of the small crew working days with bike runs of up to three hundred people.
  • Spints Alehouse was my first real management experience and introduction to creating specials and controlling inventory.
  • Interurban helped me refine my mangement and cooking style over my almost six years of being sous chef.
  • Palomar was my keystone. Starting from designing the kitchen layout, menu planning and design I was able to create a kitchen program that was simultaniously functional, welcoming, and fun to be part of.
